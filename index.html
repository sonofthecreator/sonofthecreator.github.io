Welcome To Son Of The Creator Art Gallery

“My artwork invites viewers to question the boundaries of perception, blending realism with symbolic elements to emphasize themes of introspection, surveillance, and human connection. Through textured portraits and thought-provoking juxtapositions of text and imagery, I explore the intersections of personal identity and the wider cultural narratives that shape us. Each piece is designed to challenge the viewer, drawing attention to the unseen forces influencing our reality.

About Me

Real Name - Obefuna Franklin .O

Date Of Birth- November 2
I am Son of the Creator, an artist committed to exploring the hidden depths of human experience. Raised in Lagos, Nigeria, my journey in art has been one of self-discovery, drawing on themes of spirituality and personal growth. Each piece I create is a reflection of the moments and emotions that shape our inner worlds, often portraying what words alone cannot express.

Art Journey

My art invites viewers to embark on a visual journey into introspection, where they might encounter their own questions, memories, and aspirations. Through my work, I aim to bring forward those raw emotions and quiet truths that often remain buried beneath the surface.
